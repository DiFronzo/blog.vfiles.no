My last Android phone was a cracked [Samsung Galaxy S8](https://www.gsmarena.com/samsung_galaxy_s8-8161.php) that also had a heavly burned-in keyboard layout and notification bar. I would like with this essay to give a "costumer like" review of the phone. With less of a spesification/benchmark focus and more of a day-to-day usage point of view.

## Design

The design of the phone is where it really stand out of the crowd. The recycled aluminium sides and flat OLED screen look remarkably similar to an Apple phone. The build quality is very good, and the full HD 6.55in screen is excellent, with a big, bright, crisp and super-smooth, thanks to a 120Hz refresh rate.
